Questions

Is it really invisible on screen share?

Yes. Veil uses the operating system's content-protection flag, the same mechanism that hides DRM video from screenshots. Zoom, Meet, Teams, and OBS capture the screen behind Veil, not Veil itself.

What does "bring your own key" mean?

You paste your own Anthropic (Claude) API key. Requests go straight from your machine to Anthropic — never through us. You pay Anthropic pennies per use instead of a fat monthly subscription to a middleman.

Do you see my screen or my calls?

In Own-Key mode, no — it's 100% local, nothing reaches us. In managed mode (where we run the AI so you can skip the API key), your request passes through our server to reach the AI, but we don't log or store your screen, audio, or transcripts. You pick the mode, and the app always shows which one you're in.

How is Listen mode different from just a screenshot?

Listen captures the live call audio (including the other person) and transcribes it on your machine, so Veil can suggest what to say without you typing anything.

Is this allowed?

Veil is a productivity tool for live work — sales, meetings, support, learning. Don't use it to break the rules of exams, certifications, or any platform's terms of service.

# Competitor Analysis — the real-time AI copilot market (June 2026)

Research compiled to position **Veil**. The category splits into two camps:

- **Stealth copilots** (overlay reads screen/audio, invisible on screen share) — Cluely, Final Round AI, LockedIn AI, Interview Coder, + open-source clones. This is Veil's lane.
- **Botless meeting notetakers** (transcripts/summaries, not real-time stealth coaching) — Otter, Granola, Fathom, Krisp, Convo. Adjacent, more "legitimate," not direct competitors.

## Per-competitor notes

**Cluely** — the category leader and the one to beat. Free Starter (limited), Pro ~$20/mo, **Pro + Undetectability ~$150/mo**, Enterprise ~$200/mo. Uses low-level GPU hooks (DirectX/Metal) for invisibility. Two big weaknesses Veil exploits: (1) **invisibility is paywalled at ~$150/mo** — Veil gives it free; (2) **2025 data breach exposed 83,000+ users'** transcripts and screenshots because everything runs in their cloud — Veil is local-first with no server to breach. The "cheat on everything" branding also creates professional risk; Veil positions for legitimate live work.

**Final Round AI** — strongest in interviews. ~$148/mo but only **4 sessions**, no lifetime option, cloud-based. Adds resume/cover-letter optimization and auto-apply. Narrow (interviews) and expensive per use.

**LockedIn AI** — interview copilot + coach, tech + behavioral. ~$54.99/mo unlimited or credit packs (credit anxiety is a common complaint). Cloud.

**Interview Coder** — the OG (the tool that started the Cluely founder's story). **Coding interviews only**, $799 lifetime (or ~$60/mo). Very polished stealth, but single-purpose and pricey.

**Convo** — the closest privacy-positioned competitor. Local processing on Mac, AES-256, $15–50/mo. But it's a **botless notetaker, not a stealth copilot**, and it's **Mac-only**. Veil is cross-platform and a true real-time overlay.

**Otter / Granola / Fathom / Krisp** — meeting notes and transcription, generous free tiers, broad integrations. Not real-time stealth coaches; different job.

**Open-source clones** (Natively, cheap-cluely) — prove the BYO-key + local + stealth approach works, and validate demand. They lack polish, onboarding, design, and distribution. **Veil's moat is execution + marketing, not the idea.** A clean installer, a great overlay, a 60-second onboarding, and a strong TikTok presence beat a GitHub repo every time.

## How Veil wins (the wedge)

1. **Invisible is free.** Cluely's single biggest upsell becomes Veil's default. Lead every ad with this.
2. **Local-first = no breach.** After Cluely's 83k-user breach, "nothing leaves your device" is a real, timely, defensible message — not marketing fluff.
3. **One app, six modes.** Final Round / Interview Coder do one thing. Veil covers interviews *and* sales *and* meetings *and* study *and* code — five TikTok niches, one product.
4. **No subscription.** BYO key free forever, or pay once. Directly counters subscription fatigue, the #1 complaint across all paid competitors.
5. **Cross-platform.** Convo's privacy story is Mac-only; Veil's isn't.
